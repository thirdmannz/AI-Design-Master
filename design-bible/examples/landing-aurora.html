What Prism does

One token file.
Every surface.

Figma sync

Tokens push from Figma directly to your repo. No plugin juggling, no manual exports.

Contrast validation

Every token pair is checked against WCAG AA before merge. Failing pairs are blocked.

◎

Multi-theme support

Light, dark, and high-contrast variants generated from a single semantic layer.

Framework-native output
