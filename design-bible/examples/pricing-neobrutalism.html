Questions

Is the free tier actually free?

Yes. $0 forever. No credit card required. We make money when your project grows, not before.

What counts as a deploy?

Any git push that triggers a build. Rebuilds without code changes don't count. Preview deploys count at 0.5x.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade instantly, downgrade at end of billing cycle. No locked-in contracts on monthly plans.

Do you have enterprise pricing?

Yes, for teams over 100. Contact us directly. We price by usage, not by how many times we can mention "enterprise".
