About

I've redesigned the same landing page more times than I can count. Each time I notice something I missed before. Design is not a problem you solve; it's a position you hold until a better one becomes clear.

I've been working independently since 2019. Before that, I spent six years in-house: two at a consultancy, four at a product company that no longer exists. I learned more from the failure than the consultancy.

I'm based in Oslo. I work with product companies in Europe and occasionally North America. I care about clarity, about typography that does actual work, and about design that doesn't make people feel stupid.
